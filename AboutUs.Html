About Us

Founded in 1996 in Tokyo, Japan, AR Technologies always strives to be the world's leading technology company. Through years of expertise and the skills of the founder Takashima Jun, AR has consistently stayed on the top of the ranks among technology companies. The success has made it possible for AR Technologies to start business ventures across the world, starting from a humble computer repair shop in Tokyo to a leading company having 1 million branches across the globe and 50% of electronic devices around the world using an AR Technologies product.
